# End-to-End Supply Chain Optimization (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution designed to identify inefficiencies, optimize capital allocation, and model operational improvements across three core supply chain pillars. 

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Business Intelligence:** Power BI, DAX, Power Query
* **Data Prep & Modeling:** Python, Excel
* **Methodologies:** Lean Six Sigma, 5S, Root Cause Analysis, ABC/XYZ Inventory Control

## 📊 Dashboard Pages & Business Impact

### 1. Inventory Optimization (Strategic Allocation)
* Built a dual-axis classification model (ABC/XYZ) to identify high-value, high-volatility SKUs.
* Engineered a dynamic safety stock calculation to prevent stockouts while minimizing holding costs.
* **Impact:** Modeled a reduction in dead stock by identifying 'CZ' category items for phase-out.

### 2. Warehouse Capacity & Efficiency (Lean Operations)
* Developed a spatial Heat Map integrating synthetic WMS data to identify picking bottlenecks.
* Analyzed facility utilization against an 85% maximum capacity threshold to prevent honeycombing.
* **Impact:** Proposed a slotting optimization (Kaizen event) to reduce picker travel time by relocating high-frequency items to the "Golden Zone."

### 3. Transportation & Logistics (Network Costing)
* Mapped global freight spend and evaluated carrier performance using a "Magic Quadrant" scatter plot.
* Created a predictive What-If parameter to model the financial impact of geopolitical fuel surcharges.
* **Impact:** Provided data-driven justification for contract renegotiations with carriers exhibiting high cost per mile and high late-delivery risks.
